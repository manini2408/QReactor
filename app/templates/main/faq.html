Frequently Asked Questions

What is a QR code?

QR (Quick Response) codes are two-dimensional barcodes that can be scanned using a smartphone camera. They can store various types of information such as URLs, text, contact information, and more.

How do I create a QR code?

Creating a QR code is simple:

  1. Sign up or log in to your account
  2. Click on "Generate QR" in the navigation menu
  3. Select the type of QR code you want to create
  4. Enter the required information
  5. Customize your QR code if desired
  6. Click "Generate" to create your QR code
  7. Download or save your QR code

What types of QR codes can I create?

Our platform supports various types of QR codes, including:

  • URL QR codes (link to websites)
  • Text QR codes (display plain text)
  • vCard QR codes (share contact information)
  • Email QR codes (open email clients with pre-filled fields)
  • SMS QR codes (open messaging apps with pre-filled messages)
  • Wi-Fi QR codes (connect to Wi-Fi networks)

Are the QR codes I create permanent?

Yes, the QR codes you create are permanent and will continue to work as long as the information they contain remains valid. For example, if you create a URL QR code, it will work as long as the website it links to is accessible.

Can I customize my QR codes?

Yes, our platform offers customization options for your QR codes, including:

  • Custom colors
  • Adding a logo to the center of the QR code
  • Adjusting the shape and style of the QR code elements
  • Changing the background color or adding a background image

In what formats can I download my QR codes?

You can download your QR codes in several formats, including:

  • PNG (raster image, suitable for most uses)
  • SVG (vector image, scalable without quality loss)
  • PDF (document format, ideal for printing)

Is there a limit to how many QR codes I can create?

Free accounts can create up to 10 QR codes per month. For unlimited QR code creation and additional features, consider upgrading to a premium account.
